Vale/Copper Mountain Ski Resources

Skiing is an important part of everyday life for the residents of Copper and Vale Mountains. Many small towns dot interstate 70 going through the Rocky Mountain range and they play host to seasonal sports tourists. This long tourism season requires ski supply and rental shops to dot the mountainside, providing tourists with ski rentals and equipment purchases. Those with more experience are also able to upgrade or maintain their personal equipment in most of these shops.
